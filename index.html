Fantasy Golf FAQ

Common questions about fantasy golf and the Better than Most app

What is Fantasy Golf?

Fantasy golf is a game where you select real PGA Tour golfers to form your team before each tournament. Your team earns points based on how your selected golfers perform in real tournaments. It's a fun way to compete with friends while following professional golf.

How much does Better than Most cost?

Better than Most is free to download. To play, you'll need a low-cost season pass or competition pass. These affordable passes help cover platform and data costs while keeping fantasy golf accessible for everyone.

How do I play fantasy golf?

Playing fantasy golf is easy: Download the app, create or join a fantasy golf league with friends, pick your golfers before each PGA Tour tournament, then watch your team score points based on real tournament performance!

Can I create private fantasy golf leagues?

Absolutely! Better than Most makes it easy to create private fantasy golf leagues. Simply create a league, invite your friends via a share link, and start competing together throughout the PGA Tour season.

Does the app have live fantasy golf scoring?

Yes! Better than Most provides live scoring updates during all PGA Tour tournaments. Watch your fantasy golf team's performance in real-time and see exactly where you stand on the leaderboard against your friends.

Is fantasy golf gambling?

No, Better than Most is not a gambling app. We offer fantasy golf for fun and bragging rights only. There are no cash prizes, real-money wagering, or pooled stakes. It's pure friendly competition among friends.
