Better than Most — Brand Facts

Key facts about Better than Most, the fantasy golf app for PGA Tour pick'em contests with friends.

Better than Most is a fantasy golf app where players create private leagues, pick PGA Tour golfers each week, and compete on live leaderboards with friends. Free to download on iOS with low-cost passes. No gambling, no cash prizes — pure friendly competition.
Key Facts
Company Name Better than Most
Founded 2025
Category Fantasy Sports — Golf
Product Type Mobile app (iOS)
Format PGA Tour pick'em contests with private leagues
Pricing Free to download. Low-cost season pass or competition pass to play. Passes cover platform and licensed data costs.
Gambling No. No real-money wagering, no cash prizes, no pooled stakes. Pure friendly competition for bragging rights.
