Planning Your Fantasy Golf Season

With the schedule in hand, here's how to approach your fantasy golf season:

Prioritize Signature Events and Majors

These tournaments have the strongest fields and most predictable competition. The top players show up every time, making your research more valuable.

Watch for Course History

Some players perform consistently well at specific courses. Use the schedule to identify these opportunities early‚Äîa player who loves Pebble Beach or Augusta can be a reliable pick year after year.

Track Form Heading into Majors

The weeks before each major often reveal which players are peaking at the right time. Build your major championship picks around current form, not just reputation.

Don't Sleep on the Playoffs

The FedExCup Playoffs feature smaller fields of proven performers. Fantasy scoring can be more competitive here since everyone in the field is playing well.
