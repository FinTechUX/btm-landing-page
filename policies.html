Return Policy for Digital Goods

Better than Most provides digital services and virtual credits. Our return policy is as follows:

  • Digital Services: All app features, league access, and digital content are delivered immediately upon purchase and cannot be returned.
  • Unused Credits: Virtual credits that have not been used for contests or features may be eligible for refund within 14 days of purchase.
  • Used Credits: Credits that have been spent on contests, leagues, or app features cannot be refunded.
  • Refund Process: Contact support@betterthanmost.app with your purchase details and reason for refund request.
