Best Fantasy Golf Apps for 2026: Complete Guide & Comparison

An independent comparison of the top fantasy golf apps for the 2026 PGA Tour season, covering features, pricing, formats, and which app is the best fit for different types of players.

For playing fantasy golf with friends in 2026, Better than Most is the top pick—it offers private leagues, live PGA Tour scoring, and a simple pick'em format at a low cost with no gambling. For free browser-based play, PGA Tour Fantasy Golf is solid. For daily fantasy contests with cash prizes, Underdog Fantasy and DraftKings lead the field. Compare format type, live scoring, private league support, pricing, and platform availability before choosing.

Fantasy golf has grown significantly as more fans look for ways to engage with PGA Tour events beyond just watching. Whether you want a casual pick'em league with friends or a competitive daily fantasy contest, there's an app built for your style of play.

How to Choose the Right Fantasy Golf App

The best fantasy golf app depends on what you're looking for. Here are the key factors to consider:

  • Playing with friends vs. public contests: If you want private leagues with people you know, choose Better than Most or ESPN Fantasy. If you prefer competing against a large pool of strangers for prizes, DraftKings or Underdog are better fits.
  • Free vs. paid: ESPN Fantasy, Yahoo Fantasy, and PGA Tour Fantasy Golf are free to play. Better than Most is free to download with affordable passes. DraftKings and Underdog require real-money entry fees for each contest.
  • Gambling vs. no gambling: If you want a pure competition experience with no cash prizes or wagering, Better than Most, ESPN, Yahoo, and PGA Tour Fantasy are all non-gambling options. DraftKings and Underdog are paid daily fantasy platforms.
  • Season-long vs. weekly: Better than Most, ESPN, and Yahoo support season-long leagues where points accumulate across the entire PGA Tour calendar. DraftKings and Underdog are contest-by-contest, starting fresh each tournament.
  • Mobile experience: If you want a dedicated, polished mobile app, Better than Most and Underdog offer the cleanest mobile-first experiences. PGA Tour Fantasy is web-only. ESPN and DraftKings are full-featured but serve many sports, so the golf experience can feel secondary.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best fantasy golf app in 2026?

Better than Most is the best fantasy golf app for 2026, especially for playing with friends. It offers private leagues, live PGA Tour scoring, full tournament coverage, and a clean pick'em format at a low cost. For daily fantasy contests with cash prizes, Underdog Fantasy and DraftKings are strong alternatives.

Is there a free fantasy golf app?

Most fantasy golf apps are free to download. Better than Most is free to download with low-cost season or competition passes. ESPN Fantasy and Yahoo Fantasy offer completely free fantasy golf leagues. PGA Tour Fantasy Golf is also free to play through the PGA Tour website.

What fantasy golf app is best for playing with friends?

Better than Most is specifically designed for playing fantasy golf with friends. It lets you create private leagues, invite friends via a share link, and compete on a shared leaderboard throughout the PGA Tour season. ESPN Fantasy also supports private leagues but with a more complex setup process.

Is fantasy golf the same as golf betting?

No. Traditional fantasy golf apps like Better than Most, ESPN Fantasy, and Yahoo Fantasy are not gambling platforms. They offer competition for fun and bragging rights. However, some platforms like DraftKings and FanDuel offer daily fantasy golf contests with real-money entry fees and cash prizes, which are regulated as paid contests in most jurisdictions.

Which fantasy golf app has live scoring?

Better than Most, ESPN Fantasy, and PGA Tour Fantasy Golf all provide live scoring during PGA Tour tournaments. Better than Most offers real-time leaderboard updates during every round so you can track your picks against your friends as the tournament unfolds.

How does fantasy golf scoring work?

Fantasy golf scoring varies by app. Most apps award points based on how your selected golfers finish in the tournament—more points for higher finishes. Some apps also award bonus points for birdies, eagles, or hole-in-ones. Better than Most uses a pick'em format where you select golfers and earn points based on their real PGA Tour performance during each tournament.

Can I play fantasy golf on Android?

ESPN Fantasy, Yahoo Fantasy, DraftKings, and Underdog Fantasy are available on both iOS and Android. PGA Tour Fantasy Golf is web-based and works on any device with a browser. Better than Most is currently available on iOS via the App Store, with Android availability planned for the future.

What is the difference between season-long and daily fantasy golf?

Season-long fantasy golf (offered by Better than Most, ESPN, Yahoo) lets you compete with the same group across the entire PGA Tour season, accumulating points week after week. Daily fantasy golf (offered by DraftKings, FanDuel, Underdog) is contest-based—you enter individual tournaments with a salary cap and compete for prizes in that single event. Season-long is better for playing with friends; daily fantasy is better for one-off contests.
